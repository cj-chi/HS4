Ratio-to-slider mapping (Stage 2.1)

Table: ratio_to_slider_map.json

  eye_span_to_face_width   -> eye_span        (scale 100, clamp 0-100)
  face_width_to_height     -> face_width_height
  mouth_width_to_face_width -> mouth_width
  nose_width_to_face_width -> nose_width
  eye_size_ratio            -> eye_size

Ratios are 0-1 from face landmarks; mapped value = ratio * scale + offset, clamped to default_clamp [0, 100].
Actual HS2/ABMX parameter names can be filled in when integrating with game or plugin.
